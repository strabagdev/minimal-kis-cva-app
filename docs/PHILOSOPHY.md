# Pulseframe Philosophy

## KIS-CVA

Keep It Simple.

Create. Variate. Arrange.

Every product decision must support at least one of these goals:

- Create: make or edit a groove.
- Variate: make a useful variation of a groove.
- Arrange: place grooves into a song.

If a change does not help Create, Variate, or Arrange, do not implement it.

## Product Language

Use simple language:

- Pattern Studio
- Patterns
- Arrangement
- Song

Avoid introducing new user-facing concepts unless they clearly simplify Create, Variate, or Arrange.
